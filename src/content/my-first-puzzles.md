**Akari** (also called Light Up) is a logic puzzle played on a grid of white and black cells.

- Place light bulbs in white cells.
- A bulb lights every white cell in its row and column until the light hits a black cell or the edge of the grid.
- Every white cell must be lit.
- No two bulbs may shine on each other.
- Some black cells contain numbers. Each number tells you exactly how many bulbs must be placed in the four cells orthogonally adjacent to that black cell (up, down, left, right).
